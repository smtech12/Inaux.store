Shipping Policy

At Hala Brand, we strive to provide efficient and reliable shipping services for all our customers. Please read our shipping policy carefully.

1. Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. You will receive an email confirmation once your order has been processed and shipped.

2. Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping methods:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days within major cities
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days within major cities
  • Cash on Delivery: Available for all orders within Pakistan

3. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated based on your location and order total:

  • Orders under Rs. 1000: Rs. 150 shipping fee
  • Orders Rs. 1000 - Rs. 3000: Rs. 100 shipping fee
  • Orders over Rs. 3000: Free shipping
  • Express Shipping: Additional Rs. 200

4. Delivery Timeframes

Estimated delivery times for major cities:

  • Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad: 2-3 business days
  • Faisalabad, Rawalpindi, Multan: 3-4 business days
  • Other major cities: 4-5 business days
  • Remote areas: 5-7 business days

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your order status using our order tracking page or by contacting our customer service team.

6. Undeliverable Packages

If we are unable to deliver your package due to incorrect address or recipient unavailability, we will contact you within 2 business days to arrange for redelivery or return.

7. Lost or Damaged Packages

In the rare event that your package is lost or damaged during transit, please contact us immediately. We will work with our shipping partners to resolve the issue and ensure you receive your order.

8. International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within Pakistan. We are working on expanding our shipping services to international destinations. Please check back for updates on international shipping availability.
