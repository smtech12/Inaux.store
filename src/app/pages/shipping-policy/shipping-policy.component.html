Shipping Policy

At INAUX Jewellery, we are committed to delivering your stainless steel accessories safely and efficiently. Please review our shipping policy below.

1. Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation. Once shipped, you will receive an email with tracking details for your order.

2. Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping options:

  • Standard Delivery: 3–5 business days within major cities
  • Express Delivery: 1–2 business days within major cities
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Available nationwide across Pakistan

3. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated based on your order value:

  • Orders under Rs. 2000: Rs. 200 shipping fee
  • Orders Rs. 2000 – Rs. 5000: Rs. 150 shipping fee
  • Orders above Rs. 5000: Free shipping
  • Express Delivery: Additional Rs. 250

4. Delivery Timeframes

Estimated delivery times within Pakistan:

  • Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad: 2–3 business days
  • Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan: 3–4 business days
  • Other major cities: 4–5 business days
  • Remote Areas: 5–7 business days

5. Order Tracking

Once your INAUX order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can track your shipment directly through our courier partner’s tracking system.

6. Undeliverable Packages

If delivery fails due to incorrect address or unavailability, our team will contact you to arrange redelivery. Additional charges may apply for repeated delivery attempts.

7. Lost or Damaged Packages

In the rare case of lost or transit-damaged packages, please notify us within 48 hours of delivery. We will coordinate with our courier partner to resolve the issue promptly.

8. International Shipping

Currently, INAUX ships within Pakistan only. International shipping will be introduced soon. Stay connected with us for updates on global delivery availability.
