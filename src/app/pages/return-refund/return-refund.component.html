Return & Refund Policy

At INAUX Jewellery, your satisfaction is our priority. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to assist you with a smooth return or exchange process.

1. Return Eligibility

You may request a return within 7 days of delivery if the following conditions are met:

  • Item must be unused, unworn, and in original condition
  • Original packaging, tags, and protective materials must be intact
  • No scratches, damage, or signs of wear
  • Proof of purchase or order number must be provided
  • Customized or engraved jewellery cannot be returned unless defective

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return or exchange:

  • Customized or engraved jewellery
  • Items marked as final sale
  • Used or worn jewellery
  • Items purchased during clearance promotions
  • Gift cards or store credit vouchers

3. How to Initiate a Return

To request a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer support within 7 days of delivery
  2. Share your order number and reason for return
  3. Receive return authorization from our team
  4. Securely pack the jewellery in its original packaging
  5. Ship the item to the address provided by our support team

4. Refund Process

Refunds are processed as follows:

  • Inspection Period: Within 2 business days of receiving the return
  • Refund Method: Same as original payment method
  • Card Payments: 5–7 business days processing time
  • Cash on Delivery: Refund via bank transfer or store credit

5. Exchange Policy

If you wish to exchange your jewellery:

  • Exchange request must be made within 7 days
  • Exchanges are subject to stock availability
  • Price difference will be adjusted accordingly
  • Customer bears shipping costs for exchange items

6. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect product:

  • Notify us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide clear photos as proof
  • We will arrange a replacement or full refund
  • No return shipping charges for verified cases
  • Replacement shipping will be covered by INAUX

7. Return Shipping Costs

Return shipping responsibility:

  • Defective or Incorrect Items: Free return shipping
  • Customer Preference Returns: Customer pays return shipping
  • Exchanges: Customer covers both-way shipping
  • COD Refunds: Processing charges may apply

8. Policy Exceptions

INAUX reserves the right to refuse returns if:

  • Return request exceeds the 7-day window
  • Jewellery shows signs of wear, scratches, or damage
  • Original packaging is missing
  • Policy misuse or fraudulent activity is suspected
