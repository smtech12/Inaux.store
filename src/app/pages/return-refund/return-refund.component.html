Return & Refund Policy

At Hala Brand, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not satisfied with your order, we're here to help.

1. Return Eligibility

You may return items within 7 days of delivery if they meet the following conditions:

  • Items must be unused, unworn, and in the same condition as received
  • All original tags and packaging must be intact
  • Items must be free from any damage, stains, or alterations
  • Original receipt or proof of purchase must be provided
  • Customized or personalized items cannot be returned unless defective

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Customized or personalized items
  • Items marked as final sale
  • Undergarments and intimate apparel
  • Items purchased during promotional sales
  • Gift cards and vouchers

3. How to Initiate a Return

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team within 7 days of delivery
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Our team will provide you with a return authorization
  4. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  5. Ship the item to the address provided by our team

4. Refund Process

Refunds are processed according to the following timeline:

  • Refund Initiation: Within 2 business days of receiving returned item
  • Refund Method: Same as original payment method
  • Processing Time: 5-7 business days for credit/debit cards
  • Cash on Delivery: Refund via bank transfer or store credit

5. Exchange Policy

If you prefer to exchange your item instead of returning it:

  • Exchanges must be initiated within 7 days of delivery
  • Price difference will be charged or refunded accordingly
  • Customer is responsible for shipping costs on exchanged items
  • Exchanges are subject to item availability

6. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item:

  • Contact us immediately upon discovery
  • Provide clear photos of the damage or incorrect item
  • We will arrange for a replacement or full refund
  • No return shipping costs will be charged to you
  • Replacement will be shipped at our expense

7. Shipping Costs for Returns

Return shipping costs are handled as follows:

  • Defective or Incorrect Items: Free return shipping
  • Customer Preference Returns: Customer bears return shipping costs
  • Exchange Items: Customer bears shipping for both directions
  • Cash on Delivery Refunds: May incur processing fees

8. Policy Exceptions

We reserve the right to refuse returns or exchanges if:

  • Return request is made after the 7-day period
  • Item shows signs of excessive wear or damage
  • Original packaging is missing or damaged
  • Return policy is abused or manipulated
