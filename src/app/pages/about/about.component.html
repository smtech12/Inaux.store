About INAUX Jewellery

Welcome to INAUX, where premium engineering meets high-fashion aesthetics. Our jewellery is more than an accessory — it is a statement of strength, resilience, and refinement.

Our Story

INAUX was created to redefine modern jewellery using the highest grade 316L Stainless Steel. We blend durability with elegance to craft pieces that transition effortlessly from the boardroom to the weekend. Every design reflects confidence, minimalism, and timeless appeal for both men and women.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple — to deliver long-lasting, skin-safe, and maintenance-free jewellery without compromising on luxury design. We create pieces that are built to be lived in, worn daily, and admired endlessly.

The INAUX Standard

Hypoallergenic

Crafted from 100% nickel-free 316L stainless steel, safe for sensitive skin with zero irritation.

Tarnish-Proof & Waterproof

Our steel will not oxidize or fade. Sweat-proof, shower-safe, and designed for everyday wear.

Extreme Durability

Scratch-resistant and engineered to maintain its strength and shine over time.

Zero Maintenance

No polishing kits or special care needed — just wear, shine, and repeat.
